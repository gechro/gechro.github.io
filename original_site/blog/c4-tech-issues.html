Internet Privacy - Is It Possible?

March 1, 2015

One of the largest issues in the tech world, and concerning those not directly involved in tech, is internet privacy.

For years, people have been cautious about what information they put out on the internet. This ranges from something as simple as a real name to something as private as banking information.

While intial concern was about hackers gaining access to this information, the concern has grown to companies, third-parties, and even the government. To reassure the public, many websites and apps offer the ability to have a private profile, or private page. But are you really 100% private?
